--- layout: post title: 'Blame in a Blameless Culture ' date: '2017-07-12T11:28:00.000+02:00' author: John tags: modified_time: '2017-07-12T11:28:16.985+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-525051364647796957.post-4174592384627230641 blogger_orig_url: http://john-hearn.blogspot.com/2017/07/blame-in-blameless-culture.html --- One of the biggest lies in many medium to large corporations is the supposed existence of a blameless culture. What really happens is that blame is felt but left unsaid causing unspoken grudges to be held between colleges or, especially between manager and managee.
This lack of honesty is a negative influence on workplace satisfaction.
